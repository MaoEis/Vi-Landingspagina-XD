What small step are you taking today for yourself?

Vi is your gentle guide to more balance in daily life. Made for young adults who feel overwhelmed or stuck, Vi helps you check in with yourself and offers simple, personal activity suggestions based on how you feel. With four calming pillars — Movement, Mindfulness, Connection, and Skills — Vi brings small, meaningful moments into your day. No pressure, just support to feel a little better, one step at a time.
